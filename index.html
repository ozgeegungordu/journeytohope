How it works

Give hope to a child in 3 simple steps

01

Sign up by filling out the form

Sign up by filling out the required information in the form. If you are already a member, log in and start reading our children's life stories.

02

Select a child to sponsor (or the area you want to donate to)

You can see the children registered under our children category and read their stories. You can sponsor the child of your choice. If you wish, you can donate by choosing a category instead of sponsoring a single child. You can make a one-time or monthly donation.

03

Contact us regarding the status of the child you sponsor

You can contact us to be informed about the development of the children you sponsor and get information about the child's current needs.
